About Us

Gems Arts & Science College (Registration No: KL/2019/0242803, NGO-Darpan, NITI Aayog, Government of India, NDLI Club under National Digital Library of India) is a pioneer institution in the co-operative sector run by Global Education and Management Studies (GEMS) Co-operative Ltd No. M.782. The college is affiliated to the University of Calicut and recognized by the Government of Kerala.

The campus is located about 1 km from Ramapuram town on the Kozhikode-Palakkad Highway (NH 966). The college currently offers 14 Undergraduate (UG) programmes and 6 Postgraduate (PG) programmes. Spread across 10 acres with hillocks and lush paddy fields, the campus provides a serene and ideal environment for learning.

Our Mission:

  • To deliver inclusive, high-quality higher education that is affordable and accessible to all learners.
  • To strengthen academic excellence through research, innovation, skill-based learning, and industry exposure.
  • To shape socially responsible graduates with strong ethics, leadership qualities, and commitment to community development.
